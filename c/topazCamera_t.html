(Class)

topazCamera_t 



    The symbolic viewing port for the engine.
    It acts as the bridge between the rendered scene and the viewer.
    Any number of Cameras can be maintained, but only one may be used at a time.
    See graphics.h for swapping between which camera is used for the display.

topazCamera_Type

(Enumerator)

topazCamera_Type 

 The type dictates how the camera should be updated.
topazCamera_Type_Perspective3D Indicates use for 3D perspective
topazCamera_Type_Orthographic Indicates that no distortion via perspective should take place.
topazCamera_Type_Manual Indicates that no updates to the camera's view or projection matrix will be done by the camera itself and that the user be updaing the transforms on their own.
