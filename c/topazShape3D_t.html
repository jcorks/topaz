topazShape3D_TextureSlot 

 Every shape3d can have textures attached to it, which are
 used by rendering programs.
topazShape3D_TextureSlot_0 The first slot for texturing.
topazShape3D_TextureSlot_1 The second slot for texturing.
topazShape3D_TextureSlot_2 The third slot for texturing.
